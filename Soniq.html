Before we dive into the code, I need to be completely transparent with you about how web browsers interact with phone hardware, specifically regarding volume synchronization. Here is the reality: Web browsers (like Chrome or Safari on your phone) do not allow websites to read or control your phone's physical hardware volume buttons. This is a strict security feature to prevent malicious websites from hijacking your phone and blasting sound at 100%. How it actually works on mobile: * Your phone's physical volume buttons control the "Master" device volume. This will work perfectly and automatically with your player. * The volume slider in our code controls the "App" volume. * Because they are separate, moving your physical phone buttons will not move the visual slider on the screen, and vice-versa. On mobile, it is usually best to just rely on the hardware buttons, but we will leave the slider in for when you use it on a laptop.
